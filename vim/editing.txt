EDITING
=======

	* Enter insert mode

	> i: insert at cursor
	> a: append after cursor
	> I: insert at beginning of line
	> A: append to end of line

	* Open new line
	> o: below the current line
	> O: above the current line

	* Correct the token/word. Basically combines delete and insert into one step.
	> cw
	> cW

	* Correct the line(s) by clearing and then entering insert mode. Starts inserting at the current indent level.
	> cc
